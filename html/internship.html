Internship Project – HSE AI & Automation

AI & Automation Centre of Excellence (CoE)

Project Overview

During my internship with the HSE AI & Automation Centre of Excellence (CoE), I worked across digital automation, AI governance, workflow design, and RPA operations. I gained hands-on experience building automation solutions, assessing AI systems for compliance, and supporting operational teams across national healthcare services.

Work & Responsibilities

Power Automate Development

I designed and built automation workflows using Microsoft Power Automate, extracting Microsoft Forms responses, processing data, and writing structured rows to Excel tables in OneDrive for Business.

  • Microsoft Forms → Power Automate → Excel integration
  • Triggers, parsing and variable creation
  • Automation for HSE Interim Assessments

RPA Operations & Incident Support

I reviewed RPA failure cases within Dynamics 365, investigated selector issues, and supported incident lifecycle activities (Identification → Development → Testing).

AI Governance & Compliance

Worked with the HSE AI Inventory and completed structured AI assessment templates to ensure systems met national and EU AI Act requirements.

Strategic Exposure

Assisted with documentation for strategic funds and evaluated AI project prioritisation criteria.

Skills Gained

  • Automation Workflow Design
  • AI Governance & Risk Analysis
  • Microsoft Power Platform
  • RPA Support & Troubleshooting
  • Dynamics 365 Case Management
